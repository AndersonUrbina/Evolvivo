Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

At Evolvivo, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, https://evolvivo.com.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Email addresses collected through subscriptions and contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and visit duration.
  • Form Submissions: Data submitted via forms, managed through third-party services Formcarry and Zapier for automation and processing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send email content to subscribed users.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To analyze traffic using Google Analytics.
  • To display relevant ads via Google AdSense.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect analytics data and personalize ads. You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that third-party services such as Google may use cookies independently for analytics and advertising purposes.

Learn more about Google's data practices in the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to help us provide and improve our services. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Formcarry: Formcarry Privacy Policy
  • Zapier: Zapier Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics & Google AdSense: See Google Privacy Policy

Your Choices

  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link provided in each email.
  • You can disable cookies via your browser settings to limit tracking.
  • You can request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

Data Protection and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data by using secure third-party services. However, transmission of data over the internet is never completely secure. By using our website, you acknowledge this risk.
